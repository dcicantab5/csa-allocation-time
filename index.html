Statistical Methods:

  1. Circular Statistics: Statistical methods designed for cyclical data (like hours of day) where 0 and 24 are adjacent values.
  2. Mean Direction: The average time of day for arrivals, calculated using vector methods to account for the circular nature of time.
  3. Concentration Parameter (kappa): Measures how clustered the times are around the mean direction.
  4. Rayleigh Test: Tests if a sample of circular data differs significantly from a uniform distribution.
  5. Hodges-Ajne Test: Another uniformity test that is robust against various alternative distributions.
